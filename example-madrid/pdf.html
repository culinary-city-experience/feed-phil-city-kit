The 19:00 problem (and the fix)

  • Madrid kitchens mostly reopen 20:00–21:00. Sure bets at 19:00: Ponzano (2), MO (5), Casa Dani (6, Mon–Fri), La Casa del Abuelo (8), San Ginés (10), San Miguel (11).
  • For the rest: arrive 19:00, have a caña on a terrace nearby, walk in for the first seating — the most Madrid move there is.
  • A 20:30 reservation marks you as a tourist but works fine as a compromise.

Metro in one minute

  • Tarjeta Multi (€2.50 at red machines) + 10-trip Metrobús; one card validates for both of you.
  • Metro 6:00–1:30 daily. Later: night buses from Cibeles or Cabify (~€12–15 home).
  • Golden routes: L9 south to Núñez de Balboa (Salamanca + the L5 nightlife line) · L9 → Av. de América → L7 for the Ponzano cluster · L8 from Colombia = your 15-min airport link.
  • Google Maps handles Madrid transit well. Official apps: Metro de Madrid, EMT Madrid.

Home turf · Chamartín  · stops 1

Chamberí · Calle Ponzano  · stops 2, 3, 4, 5

Wander here: The verb is "ponzanear": drift bar to bar along Calle Ponzano — El Doble (no. 58) for the pre-dinner caña. With time to spare, stroll 10 min south to Plaza de Olavide, Chamberí's living room, ringed with terraces. Late jazz at Sala Clamores is a short walk; Museo Sorolla is on this side of town too.

Salamanca  · stops 6, 7

Wander here: Perfect early-evening territory: tortilla at the market, then window-shop down Serrano — or the quieter Claudio Coello / Lagasca — to the Puerta de Alcalá, and slip into Retiro for golden hour. Calle de Jorge Juan is the elegant restaurant row; Museo Lázaro Galdiano crowns Serrano at no. 122.

Centro histórico  · stops 8, 9, 10, 11

Wander here: The classic loop: Sol → San Ginés' alley → under Plaza Mayor's arches → down Cuchilleros past Botín → Cava Baja's tapas mile into La Latina. Or head east: Plaza de Santa Ana and the Huertas lanes for terraces, with Café Central (your Tuesday jazz) on C. del Prado. Sunday morning it's El Rastro.

Booking cheat-sheet

  • Book days ahead: Botín (9), Sacha (1), Sala de Despiece (3) — links on their cards.
  • Phone only: Barrera (4) — “Hola, quería reservar una mesa para dos, el jueves a las nueve de la noche.”
  • No bookings: StreetXO (7) — arrive at 20:00 opening or eat at the bar.
  • Walk-in fine: Ponzano (2), MO (5), Casa Dani (6), La Casa del Abuelo (8), San Ginés (10), San Miguel (11).
